# Privacy Policy — Mantra Japam

**Last updated:** May 24, 2026

Thank you for using Mantra Japam ("the App", "we", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

---

## 1. Who we are

Mantra Japam is a personal spiritual practice app designed to help you count mantras, track daily mala progress, and build a consistent japam practice. The app is developed and maintained by an independent developer. You can reach us at **mantrajapamapp@gmail.com**.

---

## 2. Information we collect

### 2a. Google Sign-In

When you sign in with Google, we receive the following information from Google:

- **Display name** (first name or full name)
- **Email address**
- **Google account ID** (a unique numeric identifier)

We do not receive your Google password. We do not request access to your contacts, calendar, files, or any other Google services.

Signing in is optional. You may use the tap and timer features without an account, but your progress will not be saved to the cloud or synced across devices.

### 2b. Japam practice data

When you use the App, we store the following information to sync your progress:

- **Mala counts and mantra counts** — the number of repetitions you complete each day
- **Timer sessions** — duration, start/end times, and loop settings
- **Session history** — a record of past practice sessions (date, mala count, count)
- **Timer state** — current timer progress and settings, so the timer resumes correctly after you close the App

### 2c. App preferences (local only)

The following preferences are stored only on your device using local storage and are never sent to our servers:

- Sound and vibration settings
- Timer preferences (duration, loop mode)
- Notification permission status

### 2d. Notifications

We use device notifications only for:

- **Timer progress** — a status notification showing time remaining while the timer is running
- **Completion alerts** — a notification when a timer session or mala is completed

We do not send marketing, promotional, or third-party notifications.

---

## 3. How we use your information

We use the information described above solely to:

- **Sync your progress** across devices when you are signed in
- **Restore your practice history** when you sign in on a new device
- **Display your stats** — today's count, day streak, and session history
- **Resume your timer** exactly where you left off after the App is closed
- **Improve the App** — understanding general usage patterns to make the app better

We do **not** use your data for:

- Advertising or marketing
- Selling to third parties
- Profiling or tracking across other apps or websites
- Any purpose unrelated to your japam practice

---

## 4. Where your data is stored

Practice data (mala history, timer state, session records) is stored securely on **Supabase**, a cloud database provider, using encrypted connections (HTTPS). Your data is associated with your Google account ID and is accessible only by you.

App preferences are stored locally on your device using **AsyncStorage** and do not leave your device.

---

## 5. Data retention

Your practice data is retained as long as you have an account. If you log out, your local data is cleared from the device. Your cloud data (history and session records) remains on our servers until you request deletion.

---

## 6. Data sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for commercial purposes.

The only third parties involved in data handling are:

- **Google** — for authentication (subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy))
- **Supabase** — for cloud data storage (subject to [Supabase's Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy))

These providers are used solely to operate the App and do not receive permission to use your data for their own purposes.

---

## 7. Your rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** your data — your practice history and stats are visible inside the App
- **Delete your data** — contact us at **mantrajapamapp@gmail.com** to request full deletion of your account and all associated data from our servers
- **Withdraw consent** — you may log out at any time, which clears your data from the device. Local preferences can be cleared by uninstalling the App.

To request data deletion, please email **mantrajapamapp@gmail.com** with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and the email address associated with your Google account.

---

## 8. Children's privacy

Mantra Japam is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

---

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
