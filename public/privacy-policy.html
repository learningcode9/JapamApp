Mantra Japam

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

Mantra Japam is a personal spiritual practice app. We built it to be simple, private, and focused on your practice. This page explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Who we are

Mantra Japam is an independent app for counting mantras, tracking mala progress, and building a daily japam practice. We are an independent developer and can be reached at mantrajapamapp@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

Google Sign-In

When you sign in with Google, we receive:

  • Your display name (first name or full name)
  • Your email address
  • Your Google account ID (a numeric identifier)

We do not receive your password. We do not request access to your contacts, calendar, files, or any other Google service.

Signing in is optional. You can use the tap counter and timer without an account — your progress is saved locally on your device only.

Practice data (synced to cloud when signed in)

  • Mala counts and mantra repetition counts
  • Timer sessions — duration and settings
  • Session history — date, mala count, repetition count
  • Timer state — progress saved so your timer resumes after closing the app

App preferences (local only — never sent to our servers)

  • Sound and vibration settings
  • Timer duration preference
  • Notification permission status

3. How we use your information

We use your data only to:

  • Sync your practice history across devices when you are signed in
  • Restore your history when you sign in on a new device
  • Display your daily stats, streak, and session history inside the app
  • Resume your timer exactly where you left off after closing the app
  • Improve the app based on general usage
We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to your practice.

4. Notifications

We use notifications only for:

  • Showing remaining time while your japam timer is running
  • Alerting you when a timer session or mala is completed

We do not send marketing, promotional, or third-party notifications.

5. Where your data is stored

Practice data (mala history, timer state, session records) is stored securely on Supabase, a cloud database provider, using encrypted connections (HTTPS). Your data is associated with your Google account ID and is not shared with or accessible to other users.

App preferences are stored locally on your device using device local storage and never leave your device.

6. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for commercial purposes.

The only third parties involved in handling your data are:

  • Google — for authentication only (Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Supabase — for cloud data storage (Supabase's Privacy Policy)

These providers are used solely to operate the app and do not receive permission to use your data for their own purposes.

7. Data retention

Your practice data is retained for as long as you have an account. If you log out, your data is cleared from your device. Your cloud data remains on our servers until you request deletion (see below).

8. Your rights

  • Access — your history and stats are visible inside the app at all times.
  • Delete your data — contact us to request full deletion of your account and all associated data from our servers.
  • Withdraw consent — log out at any time to clear your data from the device. Uninstalling the app removes all local data.

To request data deletion, email mantrajapamapp@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and the email address linked to your Google account. We will process your request within 14 days.

9. Children's privacy

Mantra Japam is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has used the app and provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Questions or data requests

We're happy to help with any privacy questions or deletion requests.
