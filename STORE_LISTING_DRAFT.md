## Full description
*(4000 character max — shown on the store page)*

```
Mantra Japam is a peaceful, distraction-free app for your daily spiritual practice.

Whether you're chanting 108 names, completing a Japa Mala, or following a set daily count — Mantra Japam keeps you on track with a focused, calming experience designed for serious practitioners.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
HOW TO USE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Tap Japam — Tap the circle to count each repetition. The app tracks your progress to 108 (one mala) and records each completed mala automatically. A gentle sound and vibration mark each completion.

Timer Japam — Set a practice duration (5, 10, 15 minutes or custom) and let the timer guide your session. One mala is counted when the timer completes. Enable Auto Repeat to complete up to 5 malas in a single session without lifting a finger.

Manual Japam — Log past practice sessions that you completed offline or before you started using the app.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
FEATURES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🔢 Mala counter — counts to 108 per mala, tracks multiple malas per day
⏱ Japam timer — timed sessions with sound and vibration completion alerts
🔁 Auto-repeat — complete up to 5 consecutive malas automatically
📊 Daily stats — today's count, total malas, and current day streak
📅 History — review your past sessions with dates and mala counts
🔔 Notifications — timer progress and completion alerts (even in background)
📱 Works offline — local storage keeps your progress without an internet connection
☁️ Cloud sync — sign in with Google to sync your history across devices
🎵 Completion sound — a soft Om sound marks each completed mala
📳 Haptic feedback — gentle vibration on every tap and mala completion
🌙 Auto day streak — tracks consecutive days of practice automatically

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
YOUR PRACTICE, YOUR DATA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Your progress is stored privately. We do not sell your data or show ads. Sign in with Google to back up your history and restore it on any device. Log out any time — your data stays yours.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
PERFECT FOR
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

• Daily mantra japa (any mantra or nama)
• 108-count mala practice
• Bhakti yoga and devotional chanting
• Meditation sessions with a set time goal
• Tracking spiritual commitments (sankalpa)
• Building a consistent daily sadhana habit

Mantra Japam is built with care for practitioners who want a simple, reliable tool — not an app that distracts from the practice itself.

Begin your japam. Build your streak.
```

## Competitive positioning notes

- **vs. generic meditation apps (Calm, Headspace):** More specific — built only for mantra counting, not general meditation. Lighter, no subscription.
- **vs. basic counter apps:** Adds mala structure (108), timer mode, history, streak, cloud sync.
- **vs. other japa apps:** Clean modern UI, works offline, Google sync, no ads.

**Positioning tagline:** *"The focused mantra practice app — nothing you don't need, everything you do."*
